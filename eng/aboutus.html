About Our Company

As IC Diagnostic, we focus on rapid test products, help our customers with methods of dealing with existing or emerging food safety problems, and work to provide high quality products, services and general solutions from the manufacturer to the end consumer. Our products; milk, meat and seafood, feed, grain and oil, herbs etc. It includes rapid test kits, veterinary diagnostic tests (ELISA, PCR / RT-PCR) and devices for the detection of antibiotics, toxins, pesticides and other residues in food products. As a member of AOAC (Official Association of Analytical Chemists) and IAFP (International Food Preservation Association), IC Dagnostic's products have been approved by many international laboratories.
