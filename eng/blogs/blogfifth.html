CORONAVIRUS AFFECTED PEOPLE IN EVERY WAY!

Buesseo Seniorita
September 15, 2020

Human life has changed drastically since the first day of the coronavirus emergence. We have mentioned before that coronavirus affects human life psychologically. Apart from that, the coronavirus also affected the social life of people.

HOW DOES THE CORONAVIRUS AFFECT SOCIAL LIFE?

First, the problem caused by the coronavirus epidemic is "Social Insecurity". We can explain this as follows: When people approach each other, they feel insecure because they do not know whether there is a virus or not, and they feel insecure towards the other party for fear of infection. Compulsory isolation at home has increased reliance on telephone and computer technology devices to do on all necessary, such as work and shopping. In addition, people are used to comfort because they do what they want online, so mobility has decreased.

THE CRITERIA EMPLOYERS ARE LOOKING FOR HAVE ALSO CHANGED!

As I mentioned above, in the coronavirus process, almost all jobs were done on the internet. Therefore, one of the criteria that employers are now looking for is how successful they are in doing online business.

PEOPLE HAVE USED TO ALONE!

Because of the coronavirus, most people have gotten used to loneliness as they do not communicate with many people during the isolation process at home and now have difficulty in being in crowded environments, which causes people to be asocial and introverted. "Education" is where the coronavirus affects. Schools were also closed during the coronavirus process, so students had to continue their classes with distance education.Although the effectiveness of distance education is still a matter of debate, it is an undeniable fact that it is not as efficient as face-to-face education.

POSITIVE SIDES OF THE CORONAVIRUS

"Will the epidemic have any positive side?" You may think, but when you look from the full side of the glass, you will observe that the rates of traffic accidents have decreased since most people stay at home during the coronavirus process. http://www.trafik.gov.tr/istatistikler37 (You can view it here.) At the same time, a negative situation such as environmental pollution was prevented in this process.
