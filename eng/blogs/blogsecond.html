A DANGEROUS COMMUNICABLE DISEASE: CORONAVIRUS

Buesseo Seniorita
September 15, 2020

As everyone knows, coronavirus is a contagious and deadly disease that has recently spread all over the world. While this virus is spreading so rapidly, many questions come to the minds of millions of people. Dozens of questions such as "Are there ways to protect from coronavirus,ıf they are, what measures can we take, , can we definitely prevent the transmission of coronavirus thanks to these measures?" In this article, we will try to avoid confusion by answering these questions.

HOW CAN WE PROTECT OURSELVES FROM THE CORONAVIRUS?

Of course, there are some measures that can be taken to protect against coronavirus. Wearing a mask - of course, it is necessary to change the mask within 2 hours on average - washing our hands with soap and water for 20-30 seconds, if there is no soap, cleaning our hands with disinfectant or cologne is one of the things we need to do to protect from coronavirus. One of the most important measures is "Social Distance". It is very important for people to keep a distance between them as much as possible, at least 1.5 meters, but there is a wrong idea that people generally think we only should stay away from those who test positive. however, since we do not know whether a person who has not been tested has coronavirus, we need to put as much distance between us as possible to avoid putting ourselves at risk. In order to maintain this distance, it is necessary not to be in crowded environments because the risk of contamination of the virus is higher in such environments.As long as these measures are followed, we will minimize the likelihood of developing coronavirus, even if we do not reduce it to zero.

WHERE THE RISK OF CORONAVIRUS TRANSMISSION IS HIGH

As mentioned above, the risk of coronavirus transmission in crowded environments is greater.For example,weddings, public transport, military festivities, or any party such as birthday parties are very dangerous areas if the rules of social distance are not followed.So we need to do everything we can to avoid being in these environments until the coronavirus is literally over.

WHEN WILL THE CORONAVIRUS END?

At the moment, everyone has this question in mind, but unfortunately it is not possible to give a clear answer to this question.At the moment, people infected with coronavirus are being treated with medication, but this is not the definitive solution, so scientists are in the process of producing a "vaccine" to completely eliminate the coronavirus.All we all want is for this vaccine to be found as soon as possible and no more people will die.
